About DermaVision

DermaVision is a skin disease detection website created as part of our capstone project by IHS students. Our AI-powered system helps you identify common skin conditions from uploaded images, making skin health more accessible and understandable.

Conditions We Detect

Moderate

Chickenpox

A highly contagious viral infection causing itchy, blister-like rash.

Common Symptoms:
  • Red spots or blisters
  • Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of appetite
Mild

Vitiligo

A condition causing loss of skin color in patches.

Common Symptoms:
  • White patches on skin
  • Premature graying of hair
  • Loss of skin color
Moderate

Nail Psoriasis

A condition affecting the nails, causing pitting & discoloration.

Common Symptoms:
  • Pitting of nails
  • Discoloration
  • Thickening of nails
  • Separation of nail from nail bed
Severe

Melanoma

A serious form of skin cancer that develops in melanocytes.

Common Symptoms:
  • New or unusual growth on the skin
  • Change in size, shape, or color of a mole
  • Irregular borders
  • Multiple colors within a mole
  • Itching or bleeding
